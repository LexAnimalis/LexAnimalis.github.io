Lex Animalis is a one-of-a-kind art collection on the Cardano Blockchain made possible by artificial intelligence.

ABOUT

Lex Animalis (which is Latin for "Animal Law") is an NFT collection created with state-of-the-art Artificial intelligence, setting out to enrich the digital animal kingdom on Cardano. As you can see, this collection's artwork has an oil painting aesthetic. Subsequent releases will include more animals and diverse artistic motifs. The collection consists of 10 different animals and a total of 250 NFTs.


Every NFT is a 1/1 unique art piece. We didn't take the usual approach of having a base character and applying different layers to it, each animal has its own personality, characteristics, and aspirations.


Lex Animalis hopes to become a well-known art project on Cardano. In the near future, we'll keep expanding our artwork through art airdrops, auctions, and planned mints to give you our very best creations.
